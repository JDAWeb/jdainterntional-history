Notice of Closure

As of October 2025, JDA International has closed operations after 32 years of dedicated service. This site preserves our history and achievements for reflection and reference. Thank you to our partners, donors, staff, and communities in Uzbekistan, Afghanistan, and Iraq.

Our work continues to impact lives through completed projects and ongoing resettlement support.

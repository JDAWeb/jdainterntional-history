A Legacy of Impact (1993–2025)

From post-Soviet Uzbekistan to resilient communities in Afghanistan, JDA International has worked for over three decades to provide clean water, sustainable agriculture, education, and hope.

  • Founded from Uzbekistan Bob Hedlund moves his family to Tashkent
  • 1000 fresh water wells drilled around the Aral Sea area
  • Leprosy awarness and treatment training to 500 Uzbek doctors, new medicine for patients
  • Micro finance program initiated 3000 loans provided to start small businesses
  • Afghanistan 74 wells bringing clean water to 68,000+ people
  • 200,000 farmers trained in new crops and mechanization, 30,000 women trained in sanitation and hygeiene
  • Introduced 2-wheel tractors, strawberries, and conservation agriculture
  • Led evacuation and resettlement of 600 Afghan colleagues and families in 2021-2023
  • Closing in 2025 — our work continues through partners and the people we served

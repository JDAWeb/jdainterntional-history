A Message of Thanks

For 32 years, JDA International transformed communities through faith-inspired, community-led action. We are deeply grateful to donors (including USAID), local partners, our dedicated Afghan and Uzbek teams, and volunteers. Your support built lasting change: healthier families, bountiful harvests, and safe havens for refugees. As we close, know your impact endures.

If you're a former partner, beneficiary, or donor with questions about records or legacy giving, please reach out.

A Message of Thanks

For 32 years, JDA International transformed communities through faith-inspired, community-led action. We are deeply grateful to many donors including TearFund (UK),The Leprosy Mission (UK and India), Norweigian Lutheran Mission, Help the Persecuted, ACDI/VOCA, DAI, Purdue University, ZOA, PRM, Global Refuge, IOM, many churches and friends, local partners, our dedicated Uzbek, Afghan and Iraqi teams, and volunteers. Your support built lasting change: healthier families, bountiful harvests, and safe havens for refugees. As we close, know your impact endures.

If you're a former partner, beneficiary, or donor with questions about records or legacy giving, please reach out.

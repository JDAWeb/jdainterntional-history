# Code of Conduct

**JDA International (jdainternational.org)** is a public archive preserving 32 years of humanitarian and development work in Central Asia, Afghanistan, Iraqi Kurdistan (1993–2025).

This website exists to honor the people we served, our Uabek, Afghan and Iraqi partners, and the donors and volunteers who made it possible.

### Our Pledge
We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, nationality, race, or religion.

### Expected Behavior
- Be kind and courteous
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or humanitarian setting

### Reporting
If you encounter content that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact:

**administrator**  
Email: legacy@jdainternational.org

All reports will be reviewed and handled with discretion and respect.

### Enforcement
Content that violates this code may be removed without prior notice.

Thank you for helping us keep this legacy archive a safe and respectful space for all.
